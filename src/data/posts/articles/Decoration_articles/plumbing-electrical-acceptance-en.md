# Plumbing and Electrical Construction and Acceptance Guide

## 1. Plumbing/Electrical Construction Process

### 1. Material Use Supervision
- **Wire**: Confirm use of national standard flame-retardant BV wire (e.g. ZC-BV), flame-retardant grade meets requirements (ZA > ZB > ZC), production date within 3 years.
- **Water pipe**: Must be PPR material, recommend three-layer nano antibacterial pipe (middle yellow antibacterial layer), ensure drinking water health.
- **Accessory uniformity**: Water pipe and accessories (elbows, couplers, etc.) must be same brand, otherwise affects system warranty.

### 2. Pipeline Layout Standards
- **Cold/hot water pipe spacing**: 15 cm, left hot right cold, outlets must be horizontally aligned.
- **Strong/weak current separation**: strong and weak current not in same pipe or slot; at crossings wrap with foil paper to prevent signal interference.
- **Conduit capacity**: not too many wires in a single conduit, generally 16-pipe holds about 3 wires, ensure heat dissipation and wire-pulling space.
- **Pipeline fixing**: use pipe clamps, straight section every 60–80 cm, corner every 20–30 cm.

### 3. Grooving and Piping Craft
- **Wall grooving**: load-bearing walls strictly no horizontal grooves; non-load-bearing wall horizontal groove length better not exceed 50 cm.
- **Conduit bend arc**: keep arc at turns, avoid right-angle bends, convenient for later wire change.
- **Water pipe double-eyelid fusion**: check whether PPR pipe hot-melt joint forms smooth "double-eyelid" shape, no false welding, over-melting.

### 4. Special Craft Treatment
- **Lamp wire out of pipe bend**: prevent wire retraction, ensure firm connection.
- **Drain pipe slope**: ensure floor drain is the lowest point, reasonable slope, avoid pooling.
- **Embedded pipelines**: such as TV wall embedded 50-pipe, vanity wall drainage pipe, need advance positioning and protection.

## 2. Plumbing/Electrical Final Acceptance Standards (17-item Full Checklist)

After plumbing/electrical completion, systematic acceptance must be done; recommend checking item by item against the table below:

| No. | Acceptance item | Standard & description |
|------|--------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 1 | Compare drawing and point table | Check all switches, sockets, lights, outlets consistent with design, no omission. |
| 2 | Wall grooving standard | Mainly vertical grooves; horizontal groove length: concrete wall ≤ 30 cm, non-concrete wall ≤ 50 cm. |
| 3 | Wire spec check | Lighting wire 1.5mm², ordinary socket 2.5mm², high-power appliance (AC, kitchen) 4mm². |
| 4 | High-power appliance separate circuit | Fridge, AC, monitor, etc. should have independent circuit, with leakage protection. |
| 5 | Strong/weak current box installation | Strong box 1.6–1.8m from ground, weak box about 0.5m, two boxes spacing ≥ 50cm. Weak box reserve socket. |
| 6 | Strong/weak current separate pipe/slot | Red pipe strong, blue pipe weak, strictly not same pipe/slot. |
| 7 | Conduit fixing and shielding | Pipe clamp spacing compliant, foil paper at strong/weak crossings. |
| 8 | Pipeline spacing | ≥ 1cm gap between pipes, convenient for mortar filling, prevent hollow. |
| 9 | Junction box and wiring | Conduit and bottom box connected with lock nut; 16-pipe ≤ 3 wires, 20-pipe ≤ 4 wires; no joints in conduit. |
| 10 | Network wiring | Each room reserve Cat6 cable, smart switch bottom box reserve neutral wire. |
| 11 | Hot water pipe insulation | Hot water pipe and return pipe wrapped with insulation foam, prevent condensate; pipes in ceiling fixed with hangers, spacing ≤ 80cm. |
| 12 | Lamp position and wire | Lamp wire out of pipe bend, lamp position accurate. |
| 13 | Embedded pipe | Check TV wall, basin and other embedded pipes unobstructed, correct position. |
| 14 | Shower cold/hot outlet | Horizontally aligned, center distance 15cm, left hot right cold. |
| 15 | Bathroom drainage | Check slope toward floor drain, whether trap installed, deodorization in place. |
| 16 | Water pressure test | Pressure 0.8–1MPa, stabilize 30 min, pressure drop ≤ 0.1MPa is pass. |
| 17 | Whole-house plumbing/electrical photo archive | Take panoramic photos or video, mark dimensions, convenient for later repair reference. |

## 3. Common Problems and Countermeasures

| Problem | Risk and handling suggestion |
|------------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Strong/weak current same slot or insufficient spacing | Easily causes network signal interference, must rectify separate pipe/slot. |
| Too many wires in conduit | Affects heat dissipation and wire pulling, should increase conduit count. |
| Wall horizontal groove too long | Damages wall structure, especially load-bearing wall, need cement mortar backfill and reinforce. |
| Water pipe joint fusion unqualified | Easily leaks, should cut and redo, ensure "double-eyelid" smooth and full. |
| Bathroom drainage no slope or reverse slope | Causes pooling, must adjust pipe slope, ensure smooth drainage. |

## 4. Core Principles of Supervision and Acceptance

- **Don't relax in process**
- **Don't lower standards**
- **Don't leave problems overnight**
- **Eliminate safety hazards**
