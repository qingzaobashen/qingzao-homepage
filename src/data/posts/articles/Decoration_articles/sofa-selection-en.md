# Sofa Selection Guide

## Fabric Selection

### Leather Sofa
- Look for top-grain leather: press with hand, natural leather grain appears on surface, slowly recovers after release.
- Avoid these pitfalls: eco-leather, silicone leather, microfiber leather are actually artificial leather, poor breathability and durability.
- Grade reference: top-grain < semi-aniline < full-aniline. Aniline is top-grade top-grain with few scars, finer texture, higher price.

### Fabric Sofa
- Recommended materials:
  - Suede fabric: high-end feel, wear-resistant and stain-resistant, easy daily care.
  - Cotton-linen: good breathability, natural touch, warm at home.
- Choose cautiously:
  - Tech fabric: essentially polyester cloth with coating, looks like leather at first, but soon peels and discolors, short lifespan.

## Internal Structure: The Invisible Part Is Most Critical

When buying a sofa, must pull open the bottom zipper to see inside; those without inspection opening basically have problems.

### Frame
- Choose solid wood frame: pine, birch are good, sturdy and stable, not easy to deform.
- Avoid these: eucalyptus, mixed wood, blockboard (eco-board) have low density, easily rot, poor load-bearing, are cheap sofa materials.

### Springs and Webbing
- Best combo: manganese steel serpentine spring + high-elastic webbing. Spring wire diameter preferably above 4mm, good support and rebound.
- Don't choose pure webbing structure: easily loosens over time, sit down and sink, can't get up.

### Filling
- Seat cushion sponge: density at least 40D or 45D, integral molded better. Like soft can choose high-quality down filling.
- Low-density sponge (below 30D) and doll cotton don't choose: collapse soon, seat feel worse and worse.
- Backrest: prefer sponge + down hybrid filling, both supportive and soft.

### Bottom Closure
- Fabric closure (polyester, oxford) better: breathable and dust-proof, easy to remove for repair.
- Non-woven closure don't choose: nicknamed "disposable cover", very fragile, breaks at a touch.

## Size Selection: Comfort Is the Hard Truth

- Ground clearance: at least 15 cm, convenient for robot vacuum to get in, no cleaning dead corners.
- Seat depth: at least 55 cm, so cross-legged sitting or half-lying is comfortable.
- Seat height: 45–50 cm most suitable, sitting thigh and calf form natural right angle, easy to get up.

## Brand Reference: Choose by Budget

### Leather Sofa
- Kuka Home: mainstream market brand, complete product line, OK cost-performance.
- Stella (Sidilong): mainly modern style, good design sense, mid-to-high price.
- Ricci/Decci: high-end minimalist route, good materials and design, expensive, whole-house can reach hundreds of thousands.

### Fabric Sofa
- Feiteng (Feitong): representative domestic fabric sofa brand, many channels, some brand premium.
- Elleci (Ai Yi Rui Si): old brand on par with Feiteng, similar product style and positioning.

### Functional Sofa
- Cheers (Zhihuashi): first-class cabin sofa concept, practical function, slightly heavy appearance, good cost-performance.
- LAZBOY: positioned higher than Cheers, solid materials, refined design, more expensive.

## Selection Checklist

When buying a sofa, check by these steps:

1. Ask about fabric:
   - Leather sofa: is it top-grain leather? what grade?
   - Fabric sofa: is it suede or cotton-linen? don't take tech fabric.
2. Look inside:
   - What is the sponge density? don't take below 40D.
   - What is the backrest filling? don't take doll cotton.
   - Is the frame solid wood? is the spring manganese steel serpentine spring + webbing?
   - Is the bottom closure fabric?
3. Try the size:
   - Sit on site, feel comfortable is most important, refer to the size standards above.
4. Set budget:
   - Enough budget wants quality: choose top-grain leather sofa or high-end fabric brand.
   - Pursue cost-performance: look at mid-range of mainstream brands, or find a reliable factory customization, control good materials.
   - Need functional position: directly look at professional functional sofa brands.
