## Soft Furnishing Stage
Soft furnishing isn't just buying — it's a journey of concretizing a beautiful life, turning your imagination into everyday touch.

### 1. Whole-house custom cabinetry
1. Decision method:
   - Step 1: clarify real needs, analyze members' habits and storage.
   - Step 2: decide the solution — suitable custom method and materials.
   - Step 3: assess drawbacks — budget, schedule, eco factors.
2. Two main methods:
   - Method 1: professional whole-house custom vendor handles design, material, processing, install.
   - Method 2: on-site carpenter builds, you buy materials; carpenter processes and installs.
3. Components: design + board + processing (cut, drill, edge-band) + fittings (hardware, stone) + install.
4. Comparison:
   - Vendor custom: like full-package — worry-free but pricier, mainstream.
   - Carpenter on-site: like labor-only — cheaper but more effort, only EVA edge-band (more glue, cracks).
#### 1.1 Custom design
1. Importance: fit space and item sizes; meet habits/needs; aesthetic coordination.
2. Self-planning: analyze habits/storage; consider drawers, shelves, rods; reserve built-in appliance spots; decide style, sketch or use Kujiale app.
3. Designer choice: communication and needs understanding; view real cases for style/skill.
4. Note: if self-plan detailed enough to execute, skip designer to save fee; but usually self-design can't nail fit/tolerance — recommend a designer.
#### 1.2 Boards
1. **Board (dumpling) = core (filling) + surface (skin)**.
2. **Core**: wood + glue; differs by wood powder/particle/strip/board and species; watch glue amount/type.
3. **Surface**: solid veneer, paper, plastic veneer, paint, baked paint, wood wax oil.
4. **Performance** from core and surface material/craft.
5. **Principle**: no absolute good/bad — only fit for need (budget, scene, eco).
6. **Dimensions**: eco — less glue better; formaldehyde grade (GB/T39600-2021): E1 (≤0.124mg/m³), E0 (≤0.050), ENF (≤0.025). Stability — tighter structure, less deform. Holding power — friction with nail, by wood strength/density/hardness/elasticity/grain/moisture. Moisture — density + surface waterproof; wet areas (kitchen/bath) need moisture-proof. Beauty — surface pattern, wood grain color, processing (carving, edge). Price — material/process; standard board 1.22×2.44m. Calculation: vendors use expanded area (accurate) or projected area (~4× expanded).
7. Common boards:
   - Particle (solid-wood particle / chipboard): wood particles + glue; unstable, average hold, weak moisture, mid eco, cheap; non-wet zones.
   - OSB (oriented strand / OSB): large flakes + MDI glue; better eco than particle, strong hold, stable; wall wood veneer base or carcass; slightly pricier.
   - MDF (medium fiber): wood powder + much glue; easy carving, complex patterns, unstable, weak moisture; door/back panels, not wet zones.
   - Multi-ply (solid multi-ply / plywood): wood veneers cross-laminated, 9–11 layers; less glue, good physics, strong moisture; carcass, especially wet zones; slightly pricier; too much glue deforms, rarely doors.
   - Ecoboard (blockboard / fir plywood): softwood strips glued, thin wood both sides; easy work, poor physics, average moisture; simple carcass, carpenter on-site.
   - Solid wood: multiple boards joined (not one plank); open or water-based or wood-wax oil; eco, fine feel, pricier, environment-sensitive deform; high-end.
8. Terms:
   - Solid wood: real wood, boards joined.
   - Log: complete unjoined wood, also solid sawn timber.
   - Finger-joint and straight-join: both solid wood.
   - Standard change: GB/T28202-2011 abolished; now GB/T28202-2020 no longer uses "log" — log, sawn timber, solid board all called solid-wood furniture.
9. Marketing traps:
   - "xxx solid-wood board" names are just marketing, no real solid-wood content.
   - Strict solid wood: only finger-joint and straight-join.
   - Log board: complete unjoined, barely buyable, ignore.
10. Furniture board classification:
   - Solid-wood materials: log and solid board.
   - Solid-wood furniture (new standard): mainly solid-wood materials, regardless of log; minor artificial board allowed.
   - Pure solid-wood: market term, all solid; no national definition.
   - All solid-wood (national): surface unveneered pure solid.
   - Veneered solid-wood (national): surface veneered pure solid.
   - Artificial-board furniture: particle, OSB, MDF, multi-ply, ecoboard.
11. Board type choice: particle and multi-ply preferred. Plan A (not recommended): all particle (poor moisture). Plan B (recommended): kitchen/bath carcass multi-ply, else particle. Plan C (recommended): all carcass multi-ply, all doors particle.
12. Brands: import Egger (Austria), Cleaf (Italy), Kronospan (Germany); domestic Tubao, Qiannianzhou, Moganshan, Lushuihe, Dare, Dawangye, Wanhu; local with fame.
13. Thickness: usually 18mm; backboard 9mm; mm units.
14. Surface: unfinished core = bare board; surfaced = finished board. Methods: veneer (melamine paper, PVC, PET, solid veneer; mostly melamine = paint-free) and coating (paint, baked).
15. Painting: clear vs. mixed (transparent vs. colored); keep wood grain → clear, else mixed. Open vs. closed (show vs. cover grain; distinct from clear/mixed). Water-based (water thin, eco, mainstream) vs. oil-based (banana water). Paint = synthetic resin film; wood wax oil = plant oil/wax, penetrates, eco pretty.
#### 1.3 Processing
1. Cut, drill, edge-band.
2. Edge-band (key): seals exposed core. EVA hot-melt (much glue, uneco, visible line, cracks); **PUR hot-melt: opposite, replacing EVA, good cost-performance**; laser: best, pricey. On-site carpenter less precise, usually EVA.
#### 1.4 Fittings
Hardware and countertop.
1. Hardware: hinges, clothes rods, handles, slides, straighteners, baskets, sink/faucet for kitchen/bath cabinets. Affects use — ask price. Brands: domestic DTC (recommended), Hettich-equivalent, Topstrong, Nuomi; import Blum (Austria), Grass, Hettich (Germany), Hafele, Salice (Italy).
2. Countertop: quartz only; not stainless; slab pricey (2k–3k/㎡).
3. Sink: 304 stainless undermount.
4. Hand basin: ceramic integrated, Corian, or slab.
#### 1.5 Install
1. Check boards/fittings are yours, no color diff, right size, firm smooth.
2. Protect walls/floor during install.
3. After, check doors/drawers open smooth, hinges firm.
4. Check hardware complete, undamaged.
5. Clean site.
#### 1.6 Finding custom
1. Worry-free not money-saving → directly Sofia, Oppein.
2. Money-saving → foreman's channel, but control boards/fittings.
3. Find OEM factory via map; check scale/reputation; search "how to find whole-house custom OEM."
4. Visit factory: 1) CNC cutter edges smooth no chipping; 2) edge-band white board smooth round, no glue line; 3) six-side CNC drill; 4) eco certificate.
5. Contract: clarify board brand, eco grade, hardware brand, delivery, after-sales.

### 2. Furniture
#### 2.1 Finished furniture
1. Craft and eco usually better than custom.
2. Lower custom ratio, buy finished → save.
#### 2.2 Wood
1. Finished lets ordinary people use solid wood.
2. Hardwood vs. softwood.
3. Hardwood: North American black walnut, king of North American hardwoods.
#### 2.3 Finished furniture buying
1. Solid if possible, hardwood if possible.
2. Online: Yuanshi Wood, Vissa, Primordial, Tongmaster.
3. Price high→low: walnut, cherry, oak (not rubber, which is rubber tree), ash, elm (here beech), rubber, pine (softwood collective), spruce, camphor.
4. Check structure stable, drawers/doors smooth.
5. Eco cert: E0, Japan F4 star.
6. Size fits room.
#### 2.4 Why import wood
1. China forest cover 23% (8% at founding), below world 31%; natural forest logging banned, fast-grown promoted.
2. Import wood mostly natural forest, long growth, stable, less deform.
3. Import processing/eco standards usually higher.
4. Richer species for varied styles.
5. Reasonable import use protects domestic forest, sustainable.

### 3. Textiles
#### 3.1 Curtains
1. Install: track + curtain box recommended, hidden, can be motorized.
2. Timing: track in hard stage, box reserved at ceiling.
3. Fabric: polyester (durable, blackout, wrinkle-free, easy, wind-proof, cheap; bedroom); linen (sheer, ventilated, long-life, hard-wash, shrinks; study); cotton-linen (like linen, softer, sheer, ventilated, not sun-proof, hard-wash, deforms, cost-effective; living); silk (premium, somewhat fire-resistant, pricey; high-end bedroom/living); velvet (good drape, heavy, glossy, blackout, sound-absorb, insulated, cheap, dust-catching; bedroom/living); sheer (chemical thin, ventilated, soft light; non-blackout or auxiliary).
4. Size: height <2.8m → fixed-height cloth; >2.8m → fixed-width.
5. Pleats: sheer 1:2.5; cloth >4m → 2×, <4m → 1.8×.
6. Blackout: lining or back blackout cloth if needed.
#### 3.2 Sofa
1. Don't overpay; control budget.
2. Points: fabric easy-care (tech cloth, stain-proof); seat depth 50–60cm; good rebound (press test); style coordinated.
3. Channel: online, transparent; brands Kuka, Zuo, Cheers.
4. Size fits living space with clearance.
5. Structure: stable frame (shake test).
#### 3.3 Mattress
1. Comfort: medium-firm or medium-soft, supportive and comfortable for most.
2. Structure: spring, filling, fabric.
3. Spring: pocket coil (independent, no disturb, less breath, some zones age); tied coil (supportive, breathable, disturbs); zoned (3 zones enough; more is marketing).
4. Filling: foam (cheap, soft, sags); latex (elastic, supportive, breathable, anti-mite, pricey); memory foam (body-fit, relieves pressure, poor breath, warm). Note "latex mattress" may have little latex — check thickness.
5. Fabric: breathable, skin-friendly, anti-mite (knit, Tencel).
6. Advice: try lying; brands Sleemon, Airland, DeRUCCI, Simmons, KingKoil; eco cert (China eco, EU CE).
7. Replace every 8–10 years.
### 4. Appliances
#### 4.1 Range hood
1. Airflow = suction; 20m³/min enough; static pressure 300–400Pa.
2. Side or top; closer to pot better.
3. Self-clean, one-way exhaust, easy control.
#### 4.2 Cooktop
1. Big fire; effective = load × efficiency; 5kW big, 1st-grade 66%.
2. Integrated stove harder to repair/clean.
#### 4.3 Dishwasher
1. Built-in.
2. Wash: little diff; spray (recommended) mainstream, ultrasonic high-end.
3. Dry: little diff; condensation enough, Zeolite if budget allows.
4. Capacity: 12 sets enough; bigger better.
#### 4.4 Fridge
1. Size preset; inverter not fixed.
2. Cooling: frost-free and frost-direct mixed.
3. Fresh-keep: low temp, high humidity, low oxygen; tech suffices.
4. Circulation: fan + evaporator; single whole fridge, dual (fridge/freezer separate), triple (fridge/freezer/variable); choose latter two.
#### 4.5 Washer
1. Wash: drum (hand-rub sim, high ratio, low wear, saves water costs power, for fine); pulsator (water spin friction, lower ratio, high wear, saves water costs power, for ordinary). Prefer drum.
2. Motor: BLDC brushless, quiet, long, stable.
3. Functions:杀菌 (high-temp, silver-ion, UV); dry if budget (washer-dryer combo saves space); smart (Wi-Fi, auto-dose).
4. Capacity: 3–4 people 8–10kg, 4+ 10kg+.
5. Brands: Haier, Little Swan, Siemens, Midea, Panasonic.
6. Note: measure reserved space.
#### 4.6 Dryer
1. Condensation, vent, heat-pump; only heat-pump wear-ready.
#### 4.7 Water heater
1. Liters, tank material, constant temp, zero-cold-water, exhaust.
2. 16L normal; oxygen-free copper; water-gas dual control constant; zero-cold optional; wall/balanced exhaust.
3. Gas appliance 8-year life.
#### 4.8 TV
1. Quality: TV > laser > projector (light projected).
2. Size: viewing distance (cm) ÷ 2.5 = recommended inches.
3. Resolution: 4K; 8K little content.
4. Panel: OLED pretty pricey; LCD cost-effective; QLED or Mini-LED LCD recommended.
#### 4.9 Smart lock
1. Body: mechanical (secure) or electronic (fast).
2. Unlock: fingerprint (semiconductor mainstream), password (virtual PIN for guests), card (elderly/kids), key (backup), face (premium).
3. Points: C-grade cylinder, anti-pry; stable brand; battery ≥6 months; anti-electromagnetic (small black box).
4. Brands: Kaadas, Dessmann, Loock, Xiaomi, Philips.
5. Install: confirm door thickness/type.
#### 4.10 AC
1. Type: split (single room, flexible, cheap); central (multi-room, pretty, costly); ducted (between, small layouts).
2. Capacity: 1HP 10–15㎡, 1.5HP 15–20㎡, 2HP 20–30㎡.
3. Efficiency: grade 1.
4. Functions: inverter (comfort, save); dehumidify (humid); self-clean; silent (bedroom).
5. Brands: Gree, Midea, Haier, Hisense, Daikin.
6. Install: licensed company.
#### 4.11 Appliance summary
1. Brands: fridge/washer Haier; split AC Gree; dishwasher Midea; hood/cooktop Fotile; integrated stove Mars; smart lock Kaadas.
2. Online/offline both, group buy ok.
3. Timing: holidays (618, Double 11).
4. After-sales: complete-service brands.

Soft furnishing is the home's second birth — it gives the cold building a soul and the empty room a story. Every piece of furniture, every textile, every lamp is your annotation of life.
