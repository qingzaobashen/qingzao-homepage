# Window Sealing Knowledge and Online Must-Ask Checklist

Window sealing is a major item in renovation, with a high price, involving sound insulation, safety, and energy saving, used for over a decade. Spend 20 minutes learning the basics so you have confidence when communicating with merchants.

## Step 1: Understand the Structure First
![Glass structure diagram](/images/articles/window-sealing-online-checklist/glass-structure.jpg)
![Window structure diagram](/images/articles/window-sealing-online-checklist/window-structure.jpg)
- Window structure: mainly screen window, window sash (casement/push-pull), French glass, and window frame.
- Classification: divided into PVC-steel windows, aluminum alloy windows, and broken-bridge aluminum. PVC-steel insulates but has low strength; aluminum alloy is sturdy but not soundproof; broken-bridge aluminum is the current mainstream, insulating and sturdy.

### Three Core Elements
1. **Profile**: Frame thickness (80/90/108 series etc.) is not the wider the better; high floors use 108+, generally 90 is enough. Aluminum wall thickness new national standard requires ≥ 1.8mm; the thermal break strip must be PA66 nylon, not PVC.
2. **Glass**: Accounts for 80%, directly affecting performance. Must use tempered glass (with 3C certification). For sound insulation choose insulated laminated glass; for insulation use triple-glass double-cavity or Low-E glass, preferably filled with argon in between.
3. **Hardware**: Determines smoothness of opening/closing and durability. Brands: imported like Siegenia, G-U; domestic like Kinlong, Pai Ge; Hoppe generally only makes handles.

## Step 2: Search Merchants Online, Focus on Details
- **Local platforms**: On Xiaohongshu, Douyin, Dianping search "city + window sealing", look at local bloggers' installation photos and comment discussions.
- **E-commerce platforms**: On Taobao, JD find local merchants, browse "ask everyone" and reviews with photos, focusing on measurement, installation, and after-sales feedback.

### Pitfall Avoidance Points
- Look at installation details (waterproofing, screw driving) and product details (thermal break strip code printing, glass marking), not just the filtered effect.
- Be wary of "all-inclusive" below market price; costs may be saved from profile, hardware, or glass.
- Use Tianyancha or Qichacha to verify the merchant: established 3+ years, no renovation disputes, normal operating status.

## Step 3: Ask the Right Questions to Screen Reliable Merchants
Communicate with customer service, use these specific questions to test professionalism.

### Must-Ask Checklist
1. What is the aluminum brand? (Recommended: Dongcheng, Huajian, Xingfa, Fenglu, etc.)
2. What is the overall wall thickness? (New national standard 1.8mm, suitable for most residences)
3. How many chambers do the main frame, sub-frame, and glass sash each have? (Brand main frames generally 4+ chambers)
4. What screen window is standard? (Recommend high-definition mesh, dense and durable)
5. Which factory's glass raw sheet? (Recommended: Xinyi, CSG, Taiwan Glass, etc.)
6. Which glass deep-processing factory? (Small factories have backward technology, may have water-wave or self-explosion risk)
7. Does the glass come standard with argon filling? (Merchants with their own production line generally include it)
8. Is the glass thickness 5mm or 6mm? (For large glass over 3㎡ recommend 6mm)
9. How is Low-E glass and ultra-clear glass priced up? (Low-E generally 50–80/㎡)
10. What hardware brand? (Choose corresponding tier by budget)
11. What is the thermal break strip brand and model? (Must be PA66 nylon, multi-chamber structure better)
12. What is the sealant strip brand? Is it EPDM? (Recommended: Jiangyin Haida, Ningbo Xin'andong, etc.)
13. What installation auxiliary materials are used? (Glue: Wacker or Zhijiang; screws: 12cm 304 expansion screws)
14. Is the hollow aluminum spacer integrated bending or spliced? (Integrated bending is better)
15. Does old window removal include edge repair?
16. How long is the warranty? (Glass generally 5 years, hardware 10 years; best to choose whole-window warranty)
17. How many seals does the glass sash have? (3 is best)
18. Is it vertical insulation?
19. What costs does the quote include? (measurement, transport, installation, hardware, screen, caulking, etc.)
20. Is the installation team in-house or outsourced? How is waterproofing done?
21. How is after-sales handled? (glass self-explosion, fogging, etc.)

### Reading Merchant Replies
- Professional merchants answer parameters clearly, proactively explain principles, and even provide thermal break strip code photos.
- Those who only say "we are the best" or "absolutely no problem" are likely tricking you.
- Those who answer patiently, invite you to see the factory or site, and don't rush to close the deal have stronger service awareness.

Renovation is a protracted war, and window sealing is a key link. Do more homework, ask more details, and you will always find the right one.
