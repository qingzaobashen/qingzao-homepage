# Central Air Conditioner Selection and Installation Guide

## Brand and Installation Decisions
![Central AC selection](/images/articles/central-ac-selection-install/1-中央空调挑选.jpg)

1. **Brand choice**
   * Don't just look at price! Too low quote likely inferior materials, later repair cost absurdly high.
   * Mainstream brand reference:
     * Japanese: Daikin, Hitachi, Mitsubishi Electric, Toshiba, stable tech, energy-saving quiet.
     * Domestic: Gree, Midea, Haier, Hisense, high cost-performance, many service outlets.
     * American: York, Carrier, Trane, McQuay, advantage in large units and water systems.
   * Focus on whether local after-sales outlets are many, reputation how, may be more important than brand itself.
   * Confirm whether official authorized dealer, avoid parallel-imported or refurbished machines.

2. **Outdoor unit type: choose by installation condition**
   * Top air discharge: good heat dissipation, suitable for units with ample outdoor space, needs no obstruction above, suitable for rooftop, balcony or equipment platform.
   * Side air discharge: heat dissipation slightly lower than top discharge, suitable for smaller space, most common home, small depth requirement, ensure side and back ventilation.
   * Let merchant recommend based on your outdoor unit space size, ventilation and property rules.
   * Fan type: small unit low load single fan quieter more energy-saving; dual fan at high load higher efficiency, stronger heat dissipation.

3. **Technical parameters**
   * Indoor/outdoor super ratio: super ratio = outdoor unit tonnage ÷ sum of indoor unit tonnage, most central AC super ratio around 120%, some good quality can reach ~130%. If often multiple indoor units on together, note this number.
   * APF value: higher APF, higher energy grade; APF ≥ 5.0 is Grade 1.
   * Inverter type: DC inverter saves ~20% power than AC inverter, and quieter.
   * Compressor type: scroll compressor long life, quiet, stable; dual-rotor compressor stable at low frequency, energy-saving economical. Outdoor unit above 160 (HP) consider scroll, ≤ 160 consider dual-rotor.

4. **Key components**
   * Outlet material: don't use aluminum alloy (drips in winter), use ABS material, anti-condensation, no deformation, low noise. At acceptance remember touch outlet edge, many burrs rework!
   * Insulation foam: inferior foam makes AC a "electric heater". Choose B1 flame-retardant rubber-plastic, thickness 10–15mm best, fast rebound when pressed, no smell. Joints glued with special adhesive. Fast rebound good quality, flat when pinched return quickly, otherwise return. Recommended brands: Follex (Armacell), Huamei.
   * Condensate lift pump: condensate pipe slope < 1%, must install lift pump, otherwise leaks soak ceiling, repair needs ceiling removal. Pump mainly backs up installation; if slope done well, pump unnecessary, but manual operation may have error, long pipe or poor installer tech, install one necessary. Avoid external or mechanical pumps.
   * Refrigerant copper pipe and distributor: must be brand new, copper wall thickness at least 0.8mm, distributor choose original or quality accessories. Copper pipe recommended brands: Zhongjia, Feilun, Hailiang.
   * Electric auxiliary heat: electric auxiliary heat ≈ "hair dryer fan", very power-consuming, central AC with electric auxiliary heat usually means poor compressor, if sales tout "electric auxiliary heat" as selling point, run!
   * Drain pipe: PVC-U material, recommended brands: Gongyuan, Zhongcai.

## Contract Signing and Installation Prep

Write contract clearly, less quarrel later.

1. **Sign contract: complete details**
   * Write add-ons: drilling fee, high-altitude work fee, over-length copper pipe fee (usually 5–8m included), signal line extension fee, discuss in advance to avoid add-ons.
   * Product details: quote list detailed, including host, indoor unit price, insulation foam, copper pipe, distributor, condensate pipe, outlet material brand model written into contract.
   * Clarify warranty: manufacturer or installer warranty. Write terms: including whole machine and compressor warranty.
   * Hidden project warranty: hidden project warranty time very important, avoid leaking and removing wood ceiling, redoing paint big expense.
   * Equipment warranty: domestic central AC brands generally 6–10 years, joint brands about 3 years.

2. **Payment method**
   * When signing pay deposit remember pay less, generally advance payment 20%–30% of total, until indoor unit, outdoor unit, remote and other parts all arrived then pay full, don't pay without seeing goods, guard your wallet.

3. **Installation team**
   * So-called "three parts product, seven parts installation", best HVAC company has own professional installation team.
   * HVAC company establishment time: choose old HVAC company doing locally for over ten years, focused on one brand.

4. **Other notes**
   * Outdoor unit power line: basically central AC indoor unit power line borne by central AC dealer, outdoor unit power line borne by renovation company.
   * Energy data: ask same-on high/low rate, this model low/high frequency energy data, AC performance good or not needs real usage data.
   * Special unit config: open kitchen / 50㎡+ large living room, need reasonable cold/hot plan, avoid "small horse pulling big cart".

## Installation and Acceptance Points
![Central AC installation](/images/articles/central-ac-selection-install/2-中央空调安装.jpg)

1. **Indoor unit install: dust and vibration proof**
   * Dust: indoor unit must have dust protection, wrap with original film or dust cover, avoid renovation dust blocking filter.
   * Vibration: hanger with double nuts, top add damping pad, 1cm gap from ceiling, reduce indoor resonance noise.

2. **Pipe construction: watch craft**
   * Nitrogen brazing: when brazing copper pipe purge with nitrogen, prevent internal oxidation, otherwise oxide scaling damages compressor.
   * Condensate drain test: condensate pipe needs at least 1% slope, drain outlet do U-trap anti-odor, do water-fill test after install.
   * Hanger clamps: spacing 0.8–1m, otherwise causes copper pipe sag, refrigerant leak. Use stainless or galvanized, reinforce within 0.3m at turns. Choose stainless, rust-proof durable.

3. **System pressure hold and vacuum**
   * Pressure hold test: after pipe installed charge 40kg high-pressure nitrogen (about 4.0MPa), hold 72 hours, pressure not drop to be sealed well.
   * Vacuum before refrigerant, time enough (by pipe length, at least 30 min), otherwise affects cooling and compressor life.

4. **Final acceptance: check item by item**
   * Alignment: check outlet install level, aligned with ceiling (misalignment < 1cm), edge no burrs.
   * Operation test: each indoor unit normal air out? cooling/heating fast? any abnormal noise or vibration? at lowest temp running, outlet and copper pipe joint any dripping?
   * Get invoice, warranty card, installation confirmation, confirm manufacturer registered, activate official warranty.
