# Water Purifier Knowledge and Selection Guide

## 01 Understand the Whole-house Water System First
### What's in Tap Water
- Particulate impurities: rust and silt in pipes, plus various additives.
- Residual chlorine: chlorine left from disinfection, has a strange smell.
- Heavy metals: lead, mercury, etc. from industrial pollution and old pipes.
- Pathogens: invisible bacteria, viruses, insect eggs.

### The Whole-house Water System Includes
- Pre-filter: intercepts large particulate impurities, aperture 25–40 microns, intercepts sand, rust, insect eggs, etc.
- Central purifier: improves water from all faucets, aperture 5–10 microns, removes residual chlorine and odor.
- Drinking machine: also called RO membrane reverse osmosis purifier, produces directly drinkable water, aperture 0.1 nanometer, can even block viruses.
- Water softener: does not filter, softens water by ion exchange — replaces magnesium and calcium with sodium, removes scale, protects skin and appliances.

### Which Plan for Different Homes
| Your situation | Recommended plan | Why recommend |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Limited budget, just want safe drinking water | Under-sink RO reverse osmosis drinking machine | Best cost-performance, directly solves drinking water, suitable for most renovated homes |
| New home renovation, want healthy water | Pre-filter + end drinking machine | Basic combo, protects pipes and appliances, and you get clean drinking water |
| Pursue quality life, care for skin and appliances | Pre-filter + central softener + end drinking machine | Soft water makes skin smoother in shower, extends water heater and floor heating life |
| Villa/large flat, best water quality throughout | Pre-filter + central purifier + central softener + end drinking machine | Layered purification, all faucets have good water |

## 02 How to Choose Water Purification Equipment

### Pre-filter: Best Cost-performance
The most worthwhile equipment to install first. Costs little but works greatly. Installed after the water meter, filters the whole house water first, blocking sand, rust, insect eggs and other large particles, protecting pipes and all appliances.

Key selection:
- Filtration precision: choose 25–40 microns, balancing filtration effect and water flow speed.
- Mesh material: 316L stainless steel is best, more corrosion-resistant than 304.
- Flow rate: above 8 tons/hour, so water pressure does not drop.
- Flush method: backflush cleans better than straight flush; automatic backflush is more worry-free.
- Material safety: shell must be BPA-free food-grade material.
- Bonus: with pressure gauge to see water pressure; northern regions choose explosion-proof and freeze-proof.

### Central Purifier
Installed after the pre-filter, uses activated carbon to adsorb residual chlorine and odor, making water from all faucets smell and taste better.

Key selection:
- Filter layer: PP cotton + activated carbon, 5 microns, 99% chlorine removal; good ones add KDF media to remove heavy metals.
- Flow and size: choose by household population, generally 3–6 tons/hour; quite large, reserve installation space in advance.
- Maintenance cost: media replaced every 2–3 years, about 1500 yuan; ask clearly before buying.

### Central Water Softener: Skin Care, No Scale
Turns hard water into soft water; not for drinking, but for showering, washing clothes, protecting appliances. Water becomes slippery.

- Good for appliances: water heater, floor heating, dishwasher won't scale, last longer.
- Good for you: after shower skin is not tight, hair smoother, less laundry detergent used.

Key selection:
- Resin brand: Dow or Rohm and Haas resin from the US, lasts longer.
- Control valve: choose time + flow dual-control automatic valve head, saves water and salt.
- Regeneration method: dry salt regeneration better than wet salt.
- Flow rate: choose by house size, generally 1.5–2 tons/hour.

### End Drinking Machine: Drinking Water
The most critical step, directly related to whether your drinking water is safe. Installed under the kitchen sink, filters water to directly drinkable.

RO reverse osmosis is the preferred technology route, with the highest filtration precision, filtering heavy metals, viruses, bacteria, safest output.

Key selection:
- RO membrane brand: DuPont (Dow) is top, then GE, Hydranautics, Toray.
- Flow rate: 800G or 1000G+ for fast output, no waiting; 600G is cost-effective.
- Water yield: choose national Grade 1 water efficiency (≥ 65%), more water-saving.
- Total purified water: the larger the better, generally above 4000L.
- Zero stagnant water technology: solves the high TDS problem of the first glass of water after overnight, very important.
- Filter cost: ask clearly how often the full set of filters is replaced and total cost.

## 03 Pitfalls to Avoid When Deciding

1. Test your water quality first
Buy a TDS pen to test tap water, and look at the scale at the bottom of the kettle. TDS over 300, much scale, must go for RO machine.

2. Calculate total cost clearly
Don't just look at machine price; calculate the annual filter replacement cost. Some machines look cheap but filters are prohibitively expensive — that's a trap!

3. Plan installation in advance
- Space: measure the height, width, depth under the sink to ensure it fits and the cabinet door opens normally.
- Water/electric: RO machine needs power and a drain for wastewater. The sink countertop needs a ~35mm hole for the purifier faucet.
- Service: choose brand official installation, keep warranty vouchers.

## 04 Prepare in Advance During Renovation

1. Reserve enough installation space
Water purifier is generally installed under the sink; measure dimensions during renovation. If it doesn't fit when bought, that's too troublesome. If truly no space, it can be installed on the countertop, just less pretty.

2. Reserve power outlet
RO reverse osmosis purifier needs power because it has a pump to pressurize filtration. If no outlet, you can use a power strip, but the hot+cold integrated machine has high power, better to reserve an outlet directly.

3. Reserve faucet hole
A purifier faucet is installed on the sink, generally a 2.8–3.5cm hole. Some brands have special faucet sizes, ask clearly before buying. For old houses hard to drill, use a "faucet mounting plate" elsewhere, just less beautiful.

4. Don't forget water source and drain
The purifier needs to connect to water; install a tee at the angle valve. When in use there is wastewater; insert the wastewater pipe into the drain, or install a multi-function drain for beauty.

## 05 Summary

Choosing a water purifier is not that complicated; the key is to clarify your needs: do you just want safe drinking water, or want good water quality throughout the house? Is the budget limited, or pursue quality life?

- Where water quality is poor, RO machine is a must.
- Pre-filter is most worthwhile to install first, protecting the whole house pipes and appliances.
- When buying, must calculate long-term cost, don't be fooled by "low-price machines".

The annual maintenance cost of a whole-house water system is about 1800–2000 yuan; international big brands or high-end products may have higher maintenance costs. Water quality and usage frequency also affect maintenance cost — poor water quality and high usage mean filters are replaced more often, naturally increasing cost.
